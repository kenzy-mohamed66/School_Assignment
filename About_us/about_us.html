About Us

EduTask is a simple web application designed to help schools manage task assignments between administrators and teachers.

The system allows admins to create and manage tasks, while teachers can view and complete their assigned tasks.

Our Mission

Our goal is to make task management in schools more organized, efficient, and user-friendly.
